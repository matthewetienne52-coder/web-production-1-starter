Chapter 1.3: A Shameful Introduction Pt. 3

Looking back at the darkness in confusion, the boy slowly looked down to see what the voice had been referring to and what he had missed in his prior examinations of his body, and under the gaze of a second party, he began to understand what the problem was.

The boy was clearly not of this world, and had yet to experience most human emotions and stimuli, besides pain from his initial fall, and disgust from the stench of trash surrounding him.

However, neither of those experiences compared to what he had felt looking down at himself for a second time.

Shame.

Unimaginable shame.

The boy let out a deep groan.
